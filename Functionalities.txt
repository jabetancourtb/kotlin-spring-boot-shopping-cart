1.	A product must contain a name, description and price.
2.	There are two types of products, the simple product and the discounted product. 
	The price of the discounted product is the one entered divided by 2.
3.	A cart must contain the list of products associated with it,
	each product in the cart must have its corresponding quantity
	(ex: Paint 6 units, cement 3 units, etc)
	and a status, pending or completed as the case may be.

The expected functionalities are the following:
●	List of all products.
● 	Add, remove and modify products.
● 	List of all products in a cart.
● 	Add, delete and modify products to the cart.
● 	Checkout, you must return the final cost of the products in the cart
	and change its status to completed.
